My Best Movies According to Eder

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

Andrew Dufresne is an innocent man who is accused of murdering his wife. After being sentenced to life imprisonment, he is sent to Shawshank Prison in Maine.

Shrek

Long ago, in a distant swamp, there lived an ogre named Shrek. One day, his precious solitude is interrupted by a bunch of fairy tale characters invading his home. They were all banished from his kingdom by the evil Lord Farquaad.

Howl's Moving Castle

Sophie, an 18-year-old girl, works tirelessly in her late father's hat store. On one of her infrequent walks through the city, Sophie meets the magician Howl, a young man with extraordinary powers and extremely seductive.
